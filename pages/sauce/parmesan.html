Parmesan Sauce Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the flour and whisk until smooth.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk and continue whisking until the mixture is smooth and thickened.
  4. Add the Parmesan cheese and garlic powder and whisk until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  6. Serve over your favorite pasta or vegetables.
