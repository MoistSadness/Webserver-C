Marinara Sauce Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes (if using) and bring to a simmer.
  4. Reduce heat to low and let sauce simmer for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  6. Serve over your favorite pasta or use as a base for pizza sauce.
