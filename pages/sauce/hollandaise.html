Hollandaise Sauce Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • Pinch of salt and cayenne pepper

Instructions:

  1. Melt the butter in a small saucepan over low heat until it is melted and frothy, but not browned. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  2. In a heatproof bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and lemon juice until the mixture is light and frothy.
  3. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water, making sure the bottom of the bowl doesn't touch the water. Whisk the egg yolk mixture vigorously for about 3 minutes, until it has thickened and doubled in volume.
  4. Remove the bowl from the heat and slowly pour in the melted butter while whisking constantly, until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Add salt and cayenne pepper to taste.
  5. Keep the sauce warm until ready to serve by placing the bowl in a larger bowl of warm water. The sauce can be kept warm for up to 30 minutes.
