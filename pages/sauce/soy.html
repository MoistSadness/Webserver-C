Simple Soy Sauce Substitute Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup beef broth or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon molasses
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (low-sodium recommended)
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground ginger

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the beef or vegetable broth, balsamic vinegar, molasses, soy sauce, garlic powder, and ground ginger.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mixture is slightly thickened.
  3. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.
  4. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one month.
