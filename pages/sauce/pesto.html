Basil Pesto Sauce Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves, packed
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts or walnuts
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the basil, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, pine nuts or walnuts, and garlic in a food processor or blender.
  2. Pulse until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  3. If the mixture is too thick, add more olive oil a little at a time until you reach your desired consistency.
  4. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  5. Serve over your favorite pasta or use as a spread for sandwiches or bruschetta.
